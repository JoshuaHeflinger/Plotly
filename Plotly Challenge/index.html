Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This project is graphic visualization for the bacterial species that are in the subjects belly buttons. As you go through the data you will see a few things. One of those things that you'll see is the type of bacteria that each person has. The other thing that you'll see is the top 10 baceria cultures found. The last piece of information you will notice is how often the subjects wash their belly buttons.
